Post Office Digital

Duration:

Mar 2024 — May 2024

Role:

Sr. Product Designer

Background

According to The South African, About 235 South African Post Office (Sapo) branches are set to be closed.

Post office web concept look, from the left to the right

The Challenge

The ProblemThe closure of 235 South African Post Office (Sapo) branches presents a significant challenge, potentially leading to job losses and impacting postal services nationwide.

The Goal Post Office Digital, aims to address this issue by providing innovative digital solutions that streamline postal operations and enhance customer experiences. The goal is to modernize the postal service, making it more efficient, accessible, and resilient in the face of challenges such as branch closures..

01

User story

User stories served as the backbone of our development process, guiding us to understand the needs and expectations of users within the context of Post Office Digital.

02

Personas

User personas were pivotal in the design process for Post Office Digital, guiding decisions by offering insights into diverse user needs and preferences. They facilitated empathy-driven design, ensuring the resulting platform effectively catered to various user segments and their real-world requirements.

03

Information Architecture

Through thoughtful organization and seamless flow, Post office digital prioritizes user experience, guiding users towards their goals effortlessly.

04

Current Website

The current website lacks a clear hierarchy and visual coherence, making it challenging for users to navigate and comprehend information effectively. In contrast, the redesign concept for Post Office Digital employs Gestalt principles to enhance usability and user experience.

05

Visual design

Post Office Digital prioritizes clarity and user-friendly design, fostering an engaging visual environment that empowers users to seamlessly navigate parcel delivery tasks with efficiency and confidence.

06

My screens

Each interface is crafted with simplicity and practicality in mind, emphasizing clarity and user-friendly design to empower users throughout their experience with Post Office Digital.

Summary

Our proposed solution involves leveraging digital technology to streamline postal services, providing online alternatives for various postal needs and optimizing operational efficiency to mitigate the impact of branch closures on service accessibility and employee retention.
