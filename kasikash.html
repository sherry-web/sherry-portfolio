The Challenge

Limited Access to Financial Services and Education.

The Challenge

Hypothesis If we create a user-friendly fintech app that offers affordable financial services and comprehensive financial education, South Africans will be more empowered to manage their finances effectively and improve their financial well-being. We expect to see an increase in user engagement and financial literacy rates as a result.


Opportunity
South Africans often lack access to affordable credit. By offering microloans and credit-building services through the app, you can provide a valuable financial lifeline to users.

Benefits This opportunity not only addresses the credit gap but also encourages users to engage with the app regularly, as they monitor their credit progress and repay loans. It can also generate revenue through interest and fees.
