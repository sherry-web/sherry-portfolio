The Desktop app

Energon

Duration:

Nov 2020 — Jul 2021

Role:

UX/UI Designer

Challenge

Promote the use of renewable energy is by creating digital products that make it easy for individuals and businesses to adopt renewable energy sources.

01

Goal

Track your energy usage and production in real-time or Set goals and monitor your progress towards a more sustainable future.

02

Project scope overview

Project scope overview.

To address the growing need for efficient solar energy management, our concept project Energon Empowering Solar Sustainability," aims to create a comprehensive platform that empowers users to manage their solar energy usage, device charging, and energy consumption in the context of load shedding challenges faced in South Africa.

Additionally, the platform will offer valuable insights and tips to reduce energy waste and contribute to a greener future.

03

Target Audience

Homeowners and renters looking to optimize solar energy usage.

Businesses aiming to reduce operational costs and improve energy efficiency.

South African residents Individuals seeking to actively contribute to environmental conservation. And residents managing energy consumption during load shedding.

04

Prototype

Imagine a powerful tool that empowers you to effortlessly manage your energy consumption, reduce waste, and make eco-friendly choices—all while aligning with your commitment to environmental stewardship. With Energon, you can set personalized energy goals, track your progress, and discover actionable recommendations tailored to your lifestyle. ‍

It's the perfect companion for eco-conscious individuals like yourself who want to make a positive impact on the planet. Together, let's ignite a brighter, greener future with Energon.
